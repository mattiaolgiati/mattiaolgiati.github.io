About

Mattia Olgiati was born in Magenta, in 1993. He starts his musical studies at the Conservatorio “G. Cantelli” of Novara in bassoon with Diego Chenna. At the same time, he studies composition with Marco Lasagna. He furthers his composition studies at the Conservatorio “G. Verdi” in Como with Caterina Calderoni and Vittorio Zago, where he completes, in 2020, the pre-academic course.

In 2021 he attains at the Conservatorio of Como a Bachelor in Electronic Music under the guidance of Andrea Vigani and Walter Prati; two years later he obtains his Master in Electronic Music magna cum laude, studying with Walter Prati, Alessio Sabella, Sandro Mungianu.

He took part in a number of composition workshops and masterclasses, most notably with Marco Di Bari, Ivan Fedele, Helmut Lachenmann. He also participated in improvisation masterclasses with Walter Prati, Evan Parker, Giancarlo Schiaffini. In 2021 and 2022 he participated in the seminar “Live Electronics, sound and music computing” held by Alvise Vidolin and Nicola Bernardini at the prestigius Accademia Chigiana in Siena.

In 2022 one of his acousmatic works has been elected for performance at the 23rd CIM (Colloqui di Informatica Musicale) hosted by the AIMI (Associazione Informatica Musicale Italiana). He performed in Italy and Europe in different contexts, such as: Auditorium of Konservatorium Bern (CH), the festival of Teatro Menotti in Sormani (Sormany Library, Milan), festival Dialoghi Sonori (Fabbrica del Vapore, Milan), festival Diverso (Accademia Musicale Chigiana, Siena) and festival Form Silence (Accademia Musicale Chigiana, Siena), festival SpazioMusica (Cagliari). He’s co-founder of a number of experimental groups, like Hypnosis Electro Duo (voice and electronics), Liminal Duo (bassoon and electronics), and Trio Codice Armonico (bassoon, percussions, electronics).

He’s an active composer and performer of contemporary and electroacustic music.
